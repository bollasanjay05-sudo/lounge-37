Our Story

Welcome to Lounge 37

Lounge 37 Steakhouse & Burger Bar was created from a true passion for premium meat, great atmosphere, and unforgettable hospitality. Located in the heart of Riga's Old Town, in one of the city's most vibrant and iconic locations, we bring together a modern New York-inspired concept with elegant dark design, great music, stylish bar culture, an open kitchen, and an energy guests can feel from the moment they walk in.

Our philosophy is simple: no shortcuts and no compromises on quality. We work with premium Argentine Black Angus beef, carefully selected cuts known for rich flavor, tenderness, and true steakhouse character. Our burgers are made only from high-quality American beef, freshly prepared and perfectly grilled for a juicy, memorable taste in every bite.

Alongside steaks and burgers, we serve fresh fish dishes, seafood, creative starters, and plates designed to share with friends and family. The restaurant is spacious and comfortable, making it ideal for couples, groups of friends, business dinners, and larger events, with reservations available for both small and large groups.

Whether you come for an incredible steak, premium burgers, seafood, cocktails at the bar, or simply to enjoy the atmosphere, Lounge 37 Steakhouse & Burger Bar was built to deliver a modern, warm, and memorable dining experience in the heart of Old Town Riga.
